                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Summer is just around the corner. Are you prepared for summer storms? If not, the place to learn all you need to know is the Georgia Department of Emergency Management and Homeland Security Agency (GEMA/HS). </span></p> 
                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">As the state’s preparedness, response, and recovery agency, GEMA/HS works to prevent and respond to all emergencies in Georgia.</span></p> 
                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">GEMA/HS recently </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/">updated its website</a>, improving ease-of-use and access to information.</p> 
                    <h2 dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Get Ready</span></h2> 
                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Prepare for any disaster with </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/ready-georgia">Ready Georgia</a>.</p> 
                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Launched in 2008, this program has been helping Georgians successfully plan ahead, with </span><a href="http://ready.ga.gov/make-a-plan/create-your-ready-profile/">tools</a> and <a href="http://ready.ga.gov/make-a-plan/">instructional materials</a> developed specifically for the types of disasters that are likely to strike our state.</p> 
                    <h2 dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Stay Informed</span></h2> 
                    <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">When disaster strikes, you can turn to GEMA/HS for the </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/news">lastest news</a>, including closures, shelter information, and how you can help fellow Georgians in need.</p> 
                    <h2 dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Recover </span></h2> 
                    <p dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">The agency’s job goes beyond disaster prep. After any disaster, GEMA/HS is responsible for coordinating relief efforts, including acting as the administer of federal relief funds. It coordinates </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/infrastructure-and-local-government-assistance">local government recovery efforts </a>as well as <a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/individual-assistance">individual assistance</a>.</p> 
                    <h2 dir="ltr"><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">See the Impact in Your Community</span></h2> 
                    <p><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">Through its </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/grants">grant programs</a>, GEMA/HS provides assistance to local emergency response units, on topics such as hazard mitigation, urban security, and emergency management.<br><br><span id="docs-internal-guid-d023bae1-7fff-bfe7-5e06-ed9a6b3bca83">GEMA/HS also provides </span><a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/training">training</a> to make sure your<a href="https://gema.georgia.gov/locations"> local emergency management agency</a> is ready for any challenge.</p>
