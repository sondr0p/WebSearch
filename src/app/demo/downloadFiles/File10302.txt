      <p>The Depository Library Council (DLC) to the GPO Director, upon the close of the spring 2019 Depository Library Council Virtual Meeting, began work on developing formal recommendations and commendations for the U.S. Government Publishing Office (GPO). On May 29, 2019, <a href="/file-repository/about-the-fdlp/federal-depository-library-council/council-recommendations/3916-recommendations-commendations-of-the-depository-library-council-to-the-gpo-director-spring-2019">two recommendations and one commendation</a> were presented. These are being reviewed and discussed in detail by GPO staff. Formal GPO responses are being drafted and will be shared with the FDLP community in the near future.</p>
