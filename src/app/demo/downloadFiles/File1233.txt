           <p>Bensguide.gpo.gov is a ‘dot.gov’ site, which means it comes from the U.S. Government. Government sites that end in .gov have to follow&nbsp;<a href="https://www.consumer.ftc.gov/articles/0031-protecting-your-childs-privacy-online" target="_blank" rel="noopener">special rules for kid-designed sites</a>&nbsp;to protect privacy, but non-government sites (.com, .info, etc.) do not have to follow those rules. Some links on Ben’s Guide may take you to a non-government site. If you enter a site that asks you for personal information—your name, address, phone number, or email address—talk to a responsible adult before sharing that information.&nbsp;</p>
