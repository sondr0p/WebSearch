             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p>&nbsp;</p> 
               <hr>
               <p>&nbsp;</p> 
               <p>Policy Committee hearings are the forums fo<img alt="" src="/sites/senate.ca.gov/files/pictures/commiittee_hearings_7.jpg" style="border-width: 1px; border-style: solid; border-color: initial; margin: 5px 7px; width: 266px; float: right; height: 178px;">r public input, the best place for citizens to communicate their concerns about proposed legislation. Bills are heard in <strong>Standing Committees</strong> which meet on a regular basis throughout the year. <strong>Joint Committees</strong> have membership from both houses and consider issues of joint concern.</p> 
               <p>Committee information available online includes committee membership, staff, addresses, phone numbers, meeting schedules, and policy jurisdictions.&nbsp; For more information on the <strong>Committee Process</strong>, please visit our <a href="http://senate.ca.gov/legislativeprocess">Legislative Process</a> page.</p> 
               <p>The Policy Committees of the Senate meet on a regular basis throughout the year to hear legislation.</p>
