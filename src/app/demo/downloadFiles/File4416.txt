           <h2>Natural Disaster&nbsp;Requests</h2> 
           <p>&nbsp;</p> 
           <p>If you have been impacted by a natural disaster such as the wildfires in California, and need priority service to replace a Separation Document (usually DD Form 214 or equivalent), place the word “Natural Disaster” in the "Comments" section of&nbsp;eVetRecs&nbsp;or in the "Purpose" section of the&nbsp;Standard Form (SF) 180, Request Pertaining to Military Records.</p> 
           <p>Fax your request to our Customer Service Team at&nbsp;314-801-0927.&nbsp;</p> 
           <p>(NOTE: This fax number is only for special requests, such as hurricanes and other natural disasters)</p>
