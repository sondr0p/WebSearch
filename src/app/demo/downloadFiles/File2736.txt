           <h2>Updates on Transparency, Participation, and Collaboration</h2> 
           <div class="clear"> 
            <p><strong>September 6, 2016 -&nbsp;</strong>The National Archives releases its <a href="https://usnationalarchives.github.io/opengovplan/">Open Government Plan for 2016-2018</a>. This plan is made available for the first time on Github so the public can add issues or submit edits. The public can also send their feedback to <a href="mailto:%20opengov@nara.gov"> </a><a href="mailto:webprogram@nara.gov">webprogram@nara.gov</a> . Learn more about the plan on the <a href="https://aotus.blogs.archives.gov/2016/09/06/we-want-to-hear-from-you-naras-open-government-plan-2016-2018/">AOTUS Blog</a>.</p> 
            <p class="thumb"><img alt="Open Government Plan 2016-18" class="width20 pull-left" data-additional-classes="" data-alignment="" data-frame="" data-image_width="50" src="/files/cui/registry/open-2016-m.jpg"></p> 
            <p><strong>March 29, 2016 -&nbsp;</strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong>The National Archives will host a webinar on Open Government from&nbsp;2 - 3 p.m.&nbsp;to seek suggestions from the public on what to include in the agency's fourth Open Government Plan.&nbsp; <a href="https://www.eventbrite.com/e/webinar-open-government-at-the-national-archives-registration-23818937082">Register for the webinar</a>.</p> 
           </div> 
           <div class="clear"> 
            <p class="thumb"><strong>March 15, 2016 - </strong><strong> </strong>The National Archives kicks off the development of it's fourth Open Government Plan. Check out the NARAtions Blog post, " <a href="https://narations.blogs.archives.gov/2016/03/15/share-your-ideas-for-our-next-open-government-plan/">Share your ideas for our next Open Government Plan</a>." And share your ideas on the <a href="https://historyhub.history.gov/community/open-government">Open Government Space on History Hub</a> or email <a href="mailto:webprogram@nara.gov">webprogram@nara.gov</a>.</p> 
           </div> 
           <div class="clear"> 
            <p class="thumb"><strong>March 15, 2016 -&nbsp;</strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong>The National Archives kicks off the development of it's fourth Open Government Plan. Check out the NARAtions Blog post, " <a href="https://narations.blogs.archives.gov/2016/03/15/share-your-ideas-for-our-next-open-government-plan/">Share your ideas for our next Open Government Plan</a>." And share your ideas on the&nbsp; <a href="https://historyhub.history.gov/community/open-government">Open Government Space on History Hub</a>&nbsp;or email&nbsp;<a href="mailto:webprogram@nara.gov">webprogram@nara.gov</a>.</p> 
           </div> 
           <div class="clear"> 
            <p class="thumb"><strong>March 15, 2016 -&nbsp;</strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong>The Office of Government Information Services (OGIS) at NARA hosts an event celebrating Sunshine Week.&nbsp; Learn more about the event on the&nbsp; <a href="http://foia.blogs.archives.gov/2016/03/16/ogis-celebrate-sunshine-week-2016/">FOIA Ombudsman blog</a>&nbsp;and watch the&nbsp; <a href="https://ogis.archives.gov/outreach-events/sunshine-week-2016/sunshine-week-2016-video.htm">video of the event</a>.</p> 
           </div>

            <p class="thumb"><strong>January 13, 2016 -&nbsp;</strong><strong>&nbsp;</strong>NARA launches a new pilot project from the Office of Innovation called the History Hub. This collaboration network explores new ways of connecting with and serving customers who are interested in historical topics related to the holdings of the National Archives. Visit&nbsp;the&nbsp; <a href="http://aotus.blogs.archives.gov/2016/01/13/launch-and-learn-our-new-history-hub-pilot-project/">AOTUS Blog</a>&nbsp;to learn more and the&nbsp; <a href="https://historyhub.history.gov/welcome">History Hub</a>&nbsp;to participate.</p> 
           </div> 
           <div class="clear">

Learn more on the&nbsp; <a href="http://aotus.blogs.archives.gov/2016/01/07/releasing-all-we-can-protecting-what-we-must-3/">AOTUS blog</a>.</p>
