        <div class="textimage-text"> 
         <p>MURFREESBORO, Tenn. – Nearly 400 teens from communities across Tennessee will gather at the Embassy Suites in Murfreesboro June 2 – 4, 2018 for the Tennessee Department of Health’s <strong>TNSTRONG Youth Summit</strong>, a statewide tobacco prevention conference.</p> 
         <p>TNSTRONG stands for “Tennessee Stop Tobacco and Revolutionize our New Generation” and is Tennessee’s youth-led movement to promote tobacco-free lifestyles. TNSTRONG Youth Summit participants receive training from national-level experts to empower them to serve as peer influencers and advocates for preventing tobacco and nicotine addiction in their communities.</p> 
         <p><strong>What:&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</strong>TNSTRONG Youth Summit</p> 
         <p><strong>When:</strong>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;June 2 – 4, 2019<br> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Conference opens at 3:30 p.m. June 2 and closes at 1 p.m. June 4<br> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Event schedule available at www.tn.gov/health/health-program-areas/fhw/tn-strong/tnstrong-youth-summit/redir-tnstrong-youthsummit/schedule.html</p> 
         <p><strong>Where:&nbsp;</strong> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; Embassy Suites<br> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; 1200 Conference Center Boulevard<br> &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Murfreesboro, Tennessee 37129</p>
