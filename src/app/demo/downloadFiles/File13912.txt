      <span class="noteText"> Any renewal transaction received after 5:00 p.m. will not be processed until the next business day and will not be available for online inquiry for 2 business days.      <br id="clear"> 
     <br id="clear"> 
     <p>A <b>Professional Verification</b> is a verification of a physician's license which a hospital or other credentialing organization purchases for primary source verification. This request can be made using the form below. There is a $25 fee for a Professional Verification.</p> 
     <p>A <b>Certification of License</b> is a verification of license that is to sent to another State Board. This request must be made from <a href="https://www.veridoc.org">VeriDoc.org.</a></p> 
     <p>In order to access verification information, please enter one of the two search criteria.</p> 
     <p><span class="required">*</span> Indicates required fields.</p>
