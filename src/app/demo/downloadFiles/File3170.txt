           <h1>Grants</h1> 
           <!-- shared News with NHPRC --> 
           <p>You can apply for grants to assist you with your study of Federal Records or Presidential Papers. You can also apply for grants to collect, preserve, and publish documents important to understanding American history.</p> 
           <p><em>Look for grants in the following:</em></p> 
           <h2>National Historical Publications and Records Commission (NHPRC)</h2> 
           <p><a href="/nhprc/"><img alt="NHPRC" class="pull-left width15" src="/files/nhprc/images/logo-2012/nhprc_logo-th.jpg"></a> The <strong>National Historical Publications and Records Commission (NHPRC)</strong> is the grantmaking affiliate of the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA). You can apply for grants for collecting, preserving, and publishing documents and archives.<br> <a class="moreLink" href="/nhprc/apply/program.html">Learn More</a></p> 
           <h2 class="clear">Presidential Library Grants</h2> 
           <p><a href="/presidential-libraries/about/grants.html"><img alt="Presidential Libraries" class="pull-left width15" src="/files/presidential-libraries/images/logo/nl-logo.gif"></a> 
            <!-- SHARED content with /presidential-libraries/about/grants.html /grants/--> Several private foundations associated with our Presidential Libraries provide grants-in-aid to assist researchers studying Presidential Library holdings.<br> <a class="moreLink" href="/presidential-libraries/about/grants.html">Learn More</a></p> 
           <h2 class="clear">More Federal Grants</h2> 
           <p><a href="http://www.grants.gov"><img alt="Grants.gov" class="pull-left width15" src="/files/grants/images/grants-gov-logo.gif"></a><strong>Grants.gov</strong> allows applicants to electronically find and apply for competitive grant opportunities from all Federal grant-making agencies, including NHPRC.</p> 
           <p><em>To search for NHPRC Grants on Grants.gov:</em></p> 
           <ol> 
            <li>Go to <a href="https://www.grants.gov/web/grants/search-grants.html?keywords=NHPRC">Search Grant Opportunities</a></li> 
            <li>Enter NHPRC's CFDA number <strong>89.003 </strong> in the "Search by CFDA Number" search box.</li> 
            <li>Start Search.</li> 
           </ol> 
           <p class="smaller thumb"><a href="http://www.usa.gov/"><img alt="USA.Gov" class="floatLeft spaceRight noBorder" src="/files/global-images/buttons/micro-button-usagov.gif" vspace="3"></a> There may also be other Federal grants available on <strong>USA.gov</strong>.</p>
