    <h1>#servingTN</h1> 
   </div> 
   <div> 
    <div class="tn-rte parbase"> 
     <p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;State government's role is to provide services that citizens aren't able to get on their own. In Tennessee State Government, those services are provided by dedicated people who want to&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; serve their fellow citizens. Through the #servingTN campaign, departments across Tennessee State Government are showcasing the real relationships that citizens build with state&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; employees who provide services every day.&nbsp;</p> 
     <p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;This month, departments are telling stories about how they are #servingTNatallhours. The work of Tennessee State Government doesn't begin at 9 and end at 5. Our departments work to&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;get the job done for their fellow Tennesseans, no matter the time of day. Below are just a few of those stories:</p>
