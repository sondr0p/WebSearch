       <p>The largest and possibly the most scenic adventure race in the U.S., the Michigan Adventure Race: Silver Lake Edition offers Sahara Desert-like views to the north and south, an inland ocean to the west, and beautiful Silver Lake to the east. Choose from 5 or 8-hour courses. Teams of 1-4. Both races will feature beginner-level dirt road and paved road biking, orienteering (clinic and online instruction available), and trekking along with fun paddleboarding and sandboarding. You'll never forget this adventure.&nbsp;Capacity limit of 500 and filling fast. See the register page at <a href="http://www.miadventurerace.com/">www.miadventurerace.com</a> for prices and cancellation policy.</p> 
       <p><strong>Much of the race occurs within Silver Lake State Park but it starts and finishes off site.&nbsp;</strong></p> 
       <p><em>A <a href="/recreationpassport">Recreation Passport</a> is required for vehicle entry into Michigan state parks. To make a camping reservation, visit <a href="https://www.midnrreservations.com/" target="blank">www.midnrreservations.com</a> or call 1-800-447-2757.</em></p> 
       <p><em><strong>Please Note:</strong> Dates occasionally change and events may be canceled due to inclement weather and other reasons. Please call the park to confirm dates/times prior to your arrival.</em></p>
