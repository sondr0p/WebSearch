      <h2><i>Spring 2019</i></h2> 
      <div class="row-fluid"> 
       <ul class="thumbnails">

          <div class="caption"> 
           <p>Considering renovating a historic property? This <a href="https://purl.fdlp.gov/GPO/gpo83741" target="_blank" rel="noopener">report from the National Park Service</a> offers an extensive history of wallpaper styles and technologies, and guidance for restoration projects.</p> 
          </div>

         <div class="thumbnail">

           <p>Government publications are fun for all ages! Enjoy this <a href="https://purl.fdlp.gov/GPO/gpo120962" target="_blank" rel="noopener">picture book</a> and learn about the mating patterns of mallard ducks.</p>

         <div class="thumbnail">
          <a href="https://purl.fdlp.gov/GPO/gpo92804" title="Link to Final Report on the WPA Program, 1935-43" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><img alt="3 finalreportWPA" src="/images/Historic-publications/Spring-2019/3-finalreportWPA.png" height="321" width="225" style="display: block; margin-left: auto; margin-right: auto;"></a> 
          <div class="caption"> 
           <p>The Works Progress Administration provided employment for 8,500,000 individuals over the course of the program’s eight years in operation. The <a href="https://purl.fdlp.gov/GPO/gpo92804" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Final Report on the WPA Program, 1935-43</a> chronicles projects undertaken, employment by state and project, and program expenditures.</p>
