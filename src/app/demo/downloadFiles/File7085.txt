           <h1>The National Archives on Instagram</h1> 
           <!--  --> 
           <p><img alt="The National Archives on Instagram" class="width100 space-below" src="/files/social-media/images/headers/banner-instagram.png"></p> 
           <p dir="ltr">Since 1934, The National Archives has documented American moments from wars to celebrations, Presidential dinners to first ladies, and everything in between. Connect with us on Instagram and take a glimpse at the captured moments of today!</p> 
           <p dir="ltr">Here, we’ll post pictures and videos of behind-the-scenes looks, shots from our exhibits, special events and more, all through the eyes of Instagram’s trademark vintage filters.</p> 
           <h4>What's Instagram?</h4> 
           <p>Instagram is an online photo- and video-sharing social networking service which enables users to snap pictures and videos, add retro-cool digital filters and share them on social networking sites like Facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, and Flickr.</p> 
           <p>For more information about our comment and posting policies on Instagram, please visit our <a href="/social-media/policies/instagram-comment-and-posting-policy" title="Instagram Policies of the US National Archives">Instagram policies</a>.</p>
