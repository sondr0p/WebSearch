        <h1>Add healthfinder Content to Your Website</h1>
        <br> 
       </div> 
       <div class="shadow-wide"></div> 
       <div class="content-page-box"> 
        <p>You can add healthfinder content to your website and help share evidence-based health information with the people who need it most. And it’s all completely free to use.</p> 
        <p>The best part is that you don’t have to worry about keeping the information current. When we make an update, the content will automatically update on your website.</p> 
        <p>healthfinder offers 3 kinds of free web content:</p> 
        <ul> 
         <li>Health and wellness topics</li> 
         <li>Personalized preventive services recommendations</li> 
         <li>Videos about disease prevention and health promotion</li> 
        </ul> 
        <h3>Health and Wellness Topics</h3> 
        <p>Our <a href="/HealthTopics/">Health Topics A to Z</a> content offers the most up-to-date health and prevention information available on more than 120 topics.</p> 
        <p>The information comes from multiple agencies within the U. S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), including the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the National Institutes of Health (NIH), and the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). We translate this information into actionable, plain language health content that can strengthen any digital platform. </p> 
        <p><a href="/FreeContent/HealthTopics/"><strong>Find out how to add Health Topics A to Z content to your website</strong></a>.<strong> </strong></p> 
        <h3>Personalized Preventive Services Recommendations</h3> 
        <p>With our interactive tools, you can give users personalized, evidence-based information in just a few clicks.</p> 
        <h4>myhealthfinder</h4> 
        <p>Our interactive <a href="/myhealthfinder/">myhealthfinder</a> tool gives users personalized recommendations for – and plain language information about – clinical preventive services recommended by HHS.</p> 
        <p>The recommendations and content are tailored to the age, sex, and pregnancy status entered by the users, for themselves or a loved one. Most insurance plans cover these services without cost-sharing. <br> <a href="/StayConnected/Tools.aspx"><strong>Find out how to add the myhealthfinder tool to your website</strong></a>. </p> 
        <h4>Colorectal Cancer Screening: Which test would you prefer?</h4> 
        <p>Everyone ages 50 to 75 needs to get screened for colorectal cancer, but many people have options for which type of test to get. Consumers can use this interactive shared decision-making tool to learn about their options for colorectal cancer screening.</p> 
        <p>By answering the 7 questions in this tool, users can identify their values and preferences around screening for colorectal cancer and then use this information make a screening plan with their doctor. <br> <a href="/FreeContent/HealthWidgets/#cdc-widget-en"><strong>Find out how to add Colorectal Cancer Screening: Which test would you prefer? to your website.</strong></a></p> 
        <h3>Videos about Disease Prevention and Health Promotion</h3> 
        <p>Our videos cover important disease prevention and health promotion topics, like how critical vaccines are for preventing disease.</p> 
        <p><a href="/FreeContent/Videos/">Find out how to add these videos to your website or blog.</a></p>
