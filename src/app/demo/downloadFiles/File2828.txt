      <h1>June 2019 Elevator &amp; Amusement Device Safety Board Meeting</h1> 
     </div> 
     <div class="date">
      June 4, 2019
     </div> 
     <article> 
      <div class="tn-textandimage-event">
       <div class="eventblock"> 
        <h2>Event</h2> 
        <p> June 4, 2019 </p> 
        <p> 9:00 am CST </p> 
        <hr> 
        <h2>Location</h2> 
        <p class="location">TOSHA Room 1A 220 French Landing Drive Nashville, TN 37243</p> 
        <hr> 
        <h2>Contact</h2> 
        <p> <a href="mailto:Thomas.R.Jackson@tn.gov">Thomas.R.Jackson@tn.gov</a> </p> 
       </div> 
       <!-- Add text and images below the eventblock --> 
       <div class="textimage-text"> 
        <p>The Elevator &amp; Amusement Device Safety Board is comprised of eight (8) members who share industry knowledge, expertise and hear issues concerning elevators, other conveyances as well as amusement devices. Presentations by owners, operators and industry representatives are made, and board members determine if variances should be granted or if an action is consistent with current rules and law. When appropriate, the board makes recommendations to the department to amend rules, law, etc. through the rule-making process or via legislation.</p> 
        <p>Individuals with disabilities who wish to participate in these proceedings should contact Evelyn Gaines Guzma?n ten days prior to the meeting.</p> 
        <p>Please contact Evelyn Gaines Guzma?n by phone at (844) 224-5818, fax 615-532-1355, statewide access 711, TTY/PC Users 1-800-848-0298, or e-mail <a href="mailto:Evelyn.GainesGuzman@tn.gov"><strong>Evelyn.GainesGuzman@tn.gov</strong></a>.</p> 
        <p>Servicio de Relevo en Espan?ol</p>
