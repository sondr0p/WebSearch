       <h3 class="bg-ocean-blue-500 pa2 white" id="h2">If Your Local Office is Closed</h3> 
       <p>If your local Social Security office is closed, be aware that <a href="/onlineservices/">many services are available online.</a> </p> 
       <p>If you had a scheduled appointment, please call <strong>1-800-772-1213</strong> for assistance.</p> 
       <p>You also can do business with Social Security <a href="/agency/contact/phone.html">over the phone</a> by calling <strong>1-800-772-1213</strong> (TTY number, <strong>1-800-325-0778</strong>). </p> 
       <h3 class="bg-ocean-blue-500 pa2 white" id="h3">Emergency Payments</h3> 
       <p>If you do not receive your regularly scheduled payment from Social Security as a result of severe weather or other emergencies, in most cases you can go to any open Social Security office and request an immediate payment. To find the nearest open Social Security office, call <strong>1-800-772-1213</strong> (TTY <strong>1-800-325-0778</strong>). </p> 
       <h3 class="bg-ocean-blue-500 pa2 white" id="h4">Direct Deposit Information</h3> 
       <p>With Direct Deposit, your money is safe in your bank, savings and loan, or credit union account no matter what happens to interrupt mail service. For more information or to sign up visit <a href="/deposit/">our Direct Deposit site</a>.</p> 
       <h3 class="bg-ocean-blue-500 pa2 white" id="h5">Direct Express Information</h3> 
       <p>If you do not have a bank account, you can sign up for the <a href="https://www.usdirectexpress.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Direct Express®</a> card program. With <a href="https://www.usdirectexpress.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Direct Express®</a>, deposits from federal payments are made directly to the card account. </p> 
       <h3 class="bg-ocean-blue-500 pa2 white" id="h6">Federal Holidays</h3> 
       <p>All Social Security offices are closed on <a href="/agency/holidays.html">federal holidays</a>.</p>
