           <h1>Plain Writing at the National Archives</h1> 
           <p>The National Archives is committed to improving our service to the public. We will use <a href="#law">plain language</a> in all our communications so that the public can easily understand them. You can help us comply with the <a href="#law">Plain Writing Act</a>!</p> 
           <!--
--> 
           <h2><a name="contact"></a>How You Can Help</h2> 
           <p>If you have suggestions on ways to improve our documents and/or website, we'd like to hear from you!</p> 
           <ul> 
            <li>Contact us at <strong><a href="mailto:plainwriting@nara.gov">plainwriting@nara.gov</a> </strong></li> 
           </ul> 
           <p>Please include the URL of the web page or the name of the document, and we will work to make it easier for you to:</p> 
           <div class="clear width80"> 
            <ul class="floatLeft width40"> 
             <li>find what you need</li> 
             <li>understand what you read</li> 
            </ul> 
            <ul class="floatRight"> 
             <li>use it the first time you read it</li> 
             <li>accomplish your task</li> 
            </ul> 
           </div> 
           <h2>How are we meeting the goals of the Plain Writing Act?</h2> 
           <ol> 
            <li class="spaceBelow"><strong>Our Working Group developed these tools to help employees use plain language:</strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li><a href="/open/plain-writing/checklist.html">Plain Writing Checklist</a> &nbsp; | &nbsp; <a class="pdfImage" href="/files/open/plain-writing/plain-writing-checklist.pdf">PDF</a></li> 
              <li><a id="style" name="style"></a><a class="pdfImage" href="/files/open/plain-writing/style-guide.pdf">Style Guide</a></li> 
              <li><a href="/open/plain-writing/10-principles.html">Top 10 Principles of Plain Language</a> &nbsp; | &nbsp; <a class="pdfImage" href="/files/open/plain-writing/plain-language-top-ten.pdf">PDF</a></li> 
              <li><a href="/open/plain-writing/tips/">Plain Writing Tips</a></li> 
              <li><a class="pdfImage" href="/files/open/plain-writing/toolkit-plain-writing-for-managers-supervisors.pdf">Plain Language Toolkit for Managers and Supervisors</a><br> &nbsp;</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li><strong>We've assigned staff to oversee our plain writing efforts: </strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li>Senior Official for Plain Writing: &nbsp; <a href="mailto:plainwriting@nara.gov">Maria Carosa Stanwich</a></li> 
              <li>Plain Writing Working Group: &nbsp; <a href="mailto:plainwriting@nara.gov">James Worsham</a>&nbsp;(chair)<br> &nbsp;</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li class="spaceBelow"><strong>We're training our staff to use plain language in any document that:</strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li>&nbsp;provides information about any of our services and benefits<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li class="spaceBelow">is necessary to obtain any of our benefits or services</li> 
              <li class="spaceBelow">explains how to comply with a National Archives requirement</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li class="spaceAbove"><strong>We're engaging with our staff about the quality of their communications: </strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li>National Archives employees — including archivists, contract officers, education staff, exhibit specialists, press officers, supervisors — who write or edit public information take a plain writing course.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li>Our Plain Language Team advises staff about their content.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li>National Archives staff provide quarterly progress reports based on a plain writing directive.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li><span id="inform">National Archives staff are continually informed about plain writing through internal newsletter articles, an internal web page, and blog posts on plain writing.</span><br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li>Our Plain Language Liaisons report on what staff in their units are doing to review and revise documents - <a href="/open/plain-writing/examples/">view examples of documents written in plain language</a>.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li><a name="survey"></a> <strong>We're consulting our customers about plain language:</strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li>We use surveys to ask readers if our use of plain writing has improved their experience with the National Archives, and monitor the scores to determine how well visitors are able to understand the content and accomplish their tasks.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li>Visitors to Archives.gov, the National Archives Catalog, Search, and History Hub are presented&nbsp;with a 1-10 scale and asked:&nbsp; “What is your overall satisfaction with this site?”&nbsp; The average rating by visitors who participated in the survey was 7.1.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li>We also use <a href="/metrics/">Archives.gov Metrics</a>,&nbsp;&nbsp;<a href="/digitalstrategy/personas">Personas</a>,&nbsp; and similar tools to identify customer top tasks.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li><strong>?<a name="report"></a> We report our progress and compliance with the Plain Writing Act to the 
              <!-- <a href="http://centerforplainlanguage.org/">Center for Plain Language</a> and the--> public by April 13 each year:</strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li><a href="/open/plain-writing/reports/2017-report" target="_blank">2017&nbsp;Plain Language Compliance Report </a>&nbsp;(dated April&nbsp;2018)<br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li><a href="/open/plain-writing/reports/index.html" target="_blank">Previous Reports</a><br> &nbsp;</li> 
             </ul> </li> 
            <li><a name="report-cards"></a><strong>View how we're doing: &nbsp;</strong><br> &nbsp; 
             <ul> 
              <li><a href="https://centerforplainlanguage.org/reports/federal-report-card/2018-report-card/">2018 Plain Language Report Cards</a><br> &nbsp;</li> 
              <li><a href="https://centerforplainlanguage.org/reports/federal-report-card/2017-report-card/">2017 Plain Language Report Cards</a><br> &nbsp;</li>

           <h2 class="below">What is the Plain Writing Act?</h2> 
           <p class="spaceBelow"><a name="law"></a> <a class="pdfImage" href="http://www.gpo.gov/fdsys/pkg/PLAW-111publ274/pdf/PLAW-111publ274.pdf">The Plain Writing Act of 2010</a> requires federal agencies to write "clear Government communication that the public can understand and use."</p>
