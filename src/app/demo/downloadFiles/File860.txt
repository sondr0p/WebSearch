       <h1 class="title" id="page-title"> Senate Daily Journal </h1> 
       <!--content--> 
       <div class="region region-content"> 
        <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system clearfix"> 
         <div class="content"> 
          <div id="node-63417" class="node node-page clearfix" about="/dailyjournals" typeof="foaf:Document"> 
           <span property="dc:title" content="Senate Daily Journal" class="rdf-meta element-hidden"></span> 
           <div class="content"> 
            <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden">
             <div class="field-items">
              <div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded">
               <p>The Senate Daily Journal contains the proceedings of the Senate. Such proceedings include: the title of each measure considered and the vote thereon, messages from the Governor and Assembly, reports from Senate committees, and the contents of petitions, memorials, or other papers presented to, and authorized by, the Senate.</p> 
               <ul>
                <li><a href="https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/pubSenDailyJrn.xhtml">2017-present</a></li> 
                <li><a href="http://leginfo.ca.gov/senate-journal.html">2005-16</a></li> 
                <li><a href="http://leginfo.ca.gov/legpubs-SJprior.html">2003-04</a></li> 
                <li><a href="https://archive.org/details/californiastatelibrary?&amp;and[]=creator%3A%22california.%20legislature.%20senate%22">1850-1963</a> <em>Links to the California State Library's Digital Collections Page for California Senate Journals.&nbsp;</em></li> 
               </ul>
               <h2>State Agency Reports</h2> 
               <p><em>Reports of state departments and agencies are printed monthly as an Appendix to the Senate Daily Journal.</em></p> 
               <ul>
                <li><a href="/sites/senate.ca.gov/files/2019-20_sars_through_3.19.pdf"><em>2019-20 Agency Reports (.pdf)</em></a></li> 
                <li><a href="/sites/senate.ca.gov/files/2017-18_sars.pdf"><em>2017-18 Agency Reports (.pdf)</em></a></li> 
                <li><em><a href="/sites/senate.ca.gov/files/2015-16_sar_1.pdf">2015-16 Agency Reports (.pdf)</a></em></li> 
               </ul>
