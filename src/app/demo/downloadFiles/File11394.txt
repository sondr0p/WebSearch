      <h1>Tennessee Forestry Commission to Meet</h1> 
     </div> 
     <div class="date">
      June 4, 2019
     </div> 
     <article> 
      <div class="tn-textandimage-event">
       <div class="eventblock"> 
        <h2>Event</h2> 
        <p> June 4, 2019 </p> 
        <p> 9:00 am </p> 
        <hr> 
        <h2>Location</h2> 
        <p class="location">TWRA Main Conference Room 5107 Edmondson Pike Nashville, TN</p> 
       </div> 
       <!-- Add text and images below the eventblock --> 
       <div class="textimage-text"> 
        <p><strong>NASHVILLE </strong>– The Tennessee Forestry Commission will meet June 4 at 9 a.m. CDT at the TWRA main conference room located at 5107 Edmondson Pike in Nashville, Tenn.</p> 
        <p>The agenda includes a review and approval of minutes, program updates, a strategic plan review, ad approval of commission officers, and approval of the number of seedlings the nursery will produce for the 2021 growing season.<br> <br> The meeting is open to the public.</p> 
        <p>The Tennessee Forestry Commission comprises seven members representing the public’s interests as they relate to forest resources in the state.&nbsp;The commission advises the Tennessee Department of Agriculture on forestry issues.&nbsp;For more information, contact the Division of Forestry at 615-837-5520 or visit the Commission’s webpage at <a href="https://www.tn.gov/agriculture/forests/commission.html" target="_blank" title="Commission Link">https://www.tn.gov/agriculture/forests/commission.html</a>.</p>
