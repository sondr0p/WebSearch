       <h1>TDOT Weekly Construction Report for Middle Tennessee, May 30 – June 5, 2019</h1> 
      </div> 
      <div class="date">
       Thursday, May 30, 2019 | 
       <small>04:30pm</small>
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <div class="tn-rte parbase"> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, Resurfacing on I-40 from west of Church Street to east of Arlington Avenue</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly,</strong> <strong>8PM-5AM,</strong> There will be alternating nightly lane closures on I-40 in both directions from MM 209-213 final pavement markings.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, I-440 Reconstruction from I-40 to I-24, including removing and replacing existing pavement</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>From now through July 2020, </strong>There will be intermittent lane closures in both directions on I-440 for maintenance as needed. At least one lane open in both directions.</p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM,</strong> There will be a right lane closure on SR 6 (Franklin Pike) southbound at I-65 for bridge substructure work.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, Intersection improvements on US 31A at Welshwood Drive (LM7.24) and resurfacing from Swiss Drive (LM 4.24) at Elysian Fields Rd (LM 7.52)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly, 8PM-5AM,</strong> There will be a nightly lane closure on Nolensville Road in both directions from Swiss Drive to Harding Place for thermoplastic, ADA ramps, sidewalks, and signal work.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, I-24 over Cumberland River, Woodland St, Main St joint repair</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Monday, June 3 and Tuesday, June 4, 9PM-5AM,</strong> There will be alternating lane closures on I-24 westbound in order to get field measurements of existing joints. Three lanes to remain open with occasional rolling road blocks.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, SR 1 Grading, Drainage, Signals, and Paving from Vultee Boulevard to Jupiter Drive</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-5PM,</strong> There will be a lane closure in both directions for storm drain installation and paving. At least one lane will remain open at all times.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, Bridge Replacement on Hillwood Blvd</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>From now through July 2019,</strong> Hillwood Blvd. will be closed from SR 1 (Harding Pike) to Post Road for bridge replacement. A signed detour will be in place.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON COUNTY, Resurfacing on I-24 from near Clay Lick Rd (LM 5) to west of SR 45 Old Hickory Blvd (LM 8.65)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be alternating lane closures for milling between MM 37-40. At least one lane will remain open in each direction.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>DAVIDSON/RUTHERFORD COUNTIES, I-24 SMART Corridor</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be temporary, intermittent closures of the outside lanes of I-24 in both directions between Bell Rd &amp; Sam Ridley Pkwy to move and replace rail from various locations.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>MAURY COUNTY, SR 396 Saturn Pkwy. Extension and SR 247</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM, </strong>Sign footer installing eastbound will close left lane.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>MAURY COUNTY, Construction of New Interchange on US 43 @ SR 6</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 8AM-4:30PM, </strong>US-43 and SR 166 will have intermittent lane closures for resurfacing.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>MAURY COUNTY, Widening of Beechcroft Rd from Cleburn Rd to McCormick Crossing</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 247 for widening activities.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>ROBERTSON COUNTY, SR 161 Bridge Repair</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>From now through spring 2019,</strong> The bridge at LM 9 will be reduced to one lane for repairs. Traffic will be controlled by a temporary signal.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>ROBERTSON and SUMNER COUNTY, S.I.A. Serving Project "Deepdish"</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 52 and US 31W for phase 1 construction activities. One lane will remain open.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>SMITH COUNTY, Rockfall Mitigation on SR 25</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 25 for paving operations.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>SMITH COUNTY, Resurfacing on SR 53 from SR 141/SR 264 (LM 11.04) to US 70N (SR 24) (LM 15.66)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 53 in both directions for paving activities. One lane will remain open.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>SMITH COUNTY, Resurfacing SR 96 from the Putnam County line (LM 0.0) to Putnam County line (LM 0.66)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 96 in both directions for paving activities. One lane will remain open.<a name="_GoBack" class="anchor" id="_GoBack"></a></p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong><u>SUMNER COUNTY, Retaining Wall Construction on SR 109 from Odem’s Bend to Bypass of South Gallatin</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM, </strong>There will be temporary lane closures on SR 109 in both directions for construction and possible paving. One lane will remain open.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILLIAMSON COUNTY, Resurfacing on I-65 from near Liberty Pike (LM 13.37) to south of Concord Road (LM 17.50)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be alternating lane closures in both directions on I-65 between MM 67-71 for milling.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILLIAMSON COUNTY, Resurfacing on I-840 from near Campbell Road (LM 26) to west of the bridge over the Harpeth River (LM 33.05), including bridge deck repair</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Nightly, 8PM-5AM,</strong> There will be nightly lane closures on I-840 between MM 31-39 for milling and paving.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILLIAMSON COUNTY, Grading, drainage, and paving on SR 11 at the intersection of SR 96 in Triune</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 8AM-4PM, </strong>There will be a lane closure on SR11 NB and SR96 WB for grading work.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, I-40 Widening east of SR 109 to east of I-840</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Monday, June 3 through Wednesday, June 5, 8AM-5AM, </strong>There will be lane closures on I-40 westbound.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, Widening on SR 141 from South of Spring Creek to North of Lovers Lane</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 6AM-6PM, </strong>There will be a temporary lane closure on SR 141 for construction activities. One lane will remain open in each direction.</p> 
        <p><strong><u>&nbsp;</u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, Bridge Repair on SR 26 over Sinking Creek</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Through fall 2019, </strong>SR 26 will be down to one 10' lane in each direction on the bridge over Sinking Creek. One lane will be open in each direction.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, Resurfacing from US 70 (SR26) (LM 18) to the Smith County Line (LM 27.73)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 24 in both directions for paving activities. One lane will remain open.<strong><u></u></strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, Resurfacing on US 231 from Market Street (LM 14.41) to near Forrest Avenue (LM 15.18)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM,</strong> There will be temporary lane closures on SR 10 (US 231) in both directions for paving activities. One lane will remain open.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong><u>WILSON COUNTY, Resurfacing SR 141 from East of Stokes Street (LM 10.54) to Sugar Flat Road (LM 14.59)</u></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3:30PM</strong>, There will be temporary lane closures on SR 141 in both directions for paving activities. One lane will remain open.<strong></strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&gt;&gt;&gt; MAINTENANCE &amp; UTILITIES</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>DAVIDSON COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM,</strong> There will be a lane closure on southbound SR 1 for construction of sidewalk and curb and gutter for KIPP Nashville (LM 30).</p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Thursday, May 30, 8PM-5AM,</strong> There will be lane closures on I-40 westbound at MM 197 for paving.</p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>DICKSON COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Wednesday, May 29-Friday, May 31, 9AM-3PM, </strong>There will be a cleanup of a fuel spill that will require intermittent lane closures.<strong></strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>RUTHERFORD COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Wednesday, May 29-Tuesday, June 4, 6PM-6AM,</strong> There will be alternating lane closures at I-840 in both directions at MM 55 for milling and patching.<strong></strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Thursday, June 30- Wednesday, June 5, 9AM-3PM, </strong>There will be lane closure(s) on SR 266 for miscellaneous operations<strong></strong></p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>SUMNER COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Monday, June 3, 9AM-3PM, </strong>There will be a lane closure on SR 6 northbound of milling and paving.</p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Tuesday, June 4, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be milling and paving on I-65 Northbound (MM 99.10) resulting in the closure of the slow lane.</p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Wednesday, June 5, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will Bridge Deck Repair on I-65 Southbound (MM 98.80) resulting in the closure of the fast lane.</p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Thursday, June 6, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be milling and paving on I-65 Southbound (MM 101.30) resulting in the closure of the slow lane.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>WILLIAMSON COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Monday, June 6-Tuesday, June 7, 8PM-5AM, </strong>There will be one lane closure on SR 11 at MM 15 for subgrade work.</p> 
        <p><strong>&nbsp;</strong></p> 
        <p><strong>WILSON COUNTY</strong></p> 
        <p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong>Daily, 9AM-3PM, </strong>There will be rolling roadblocks on SR 109, between Callis Road and I-40, for rock blasting/excavation. Additionally, traffic will be reduced to one lane in both directions at the intersection of SR 109 and Callis Road for construction of turn lane and crossover.</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p>Motorists are encouraged to use caution and obey reduced speed limits in all TDOT work zones, regardless of lane closure activity.&nbsp; Information in this report is provided to the Department of Transportation by the contractors.&nbsp; Most work is weather dependent and subject to change due to inclement weather.&nbsp;</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p>From your desktop or mobile device, get the latest construction activity and live streaming SmartWay traffic cameras at <a href="http://www.tnsmartway.com/Traffic">www.TNSmartWay.com/Traffic</a>. Travelers can also dial 511 from any land-line or cellular phone for travel information, or follow us on Twitter at <a href="http://www.twitter.com/TN511">www.twitter.com/TN511</a> for statewide travel.&nbsp;</p> 
        <p>&nbsp;</p> 
        <p>As always, drivers are reminded to use all motorist information tools wisely and <em>Know Before You Go! </em>by checking travel conditions before leaving for your destination.&nbsp; Drivers should never tweet, text or talk on a cell phone while behind the wheel.</p>
