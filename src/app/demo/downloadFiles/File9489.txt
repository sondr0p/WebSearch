        <h2>Get This Tool for Your Website</h2> 
        <p>There are 3 ways to incorporate personalized preventive services recommendations (myhealthfinder) into your own website or digital platform: a web badge, a widget that displays results on the hosting website, and a customizable API for developers.</p> 
        <p class="no-margin-p"><a href="/StayConnected/Tools.aspx"><strong>See how to add myhealthfinder to your website</strong></a>.</p>

The Affordable Care Act requires most insurance plans to cover these preventive services at no cost to you.</p>
