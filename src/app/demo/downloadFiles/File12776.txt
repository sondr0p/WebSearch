       <h1>Register for EDI Claims Release 3.1 Training</h1> 
      </div> 
      <div class="date">
       Monday, June 10, 2019 | 
       <small>01:00pm</small>
      </div> 
      <div> 
       <div class="tn-rte parbase"> 
        <p>The <strong><a href="https://www.iaiabc.org/assnfe/ev.asp?ID=533&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;utm_campaign=EDI%20Claims%2031%20Training%20Tennessee%20Reminder&amp;utm_content=EDI%20Claims%2031%20Training%20Tennessee%20Reminder+Preview+CID_6193b64fc752bb64084392770cd0aac2&amp;utm_source=Email%20marketing%20software&amp;utm_term=EDI%20Claims%20Release%2031%20face-to-face%20training" target="_blank">EDI Claims Release 3.1 face-to-face training</a></strong> provides a great opportunity for jurisdictions and industry participants to learn about the newest release of the IAIABC Claims Standard. Join us for this in-depth training June 10 (1:00 pm - 5:00 pm) through June 11 (8:00 am - 5:00 pm).</p> 
        <p>The EDI Claims Release 3.1 training will be held in Murfreesboro, Tennessee (SE Nashville) at the Embassy&nbsp;Suites and will include discussion of enhancements and new features of 3.1; Data Elements and Processing rules; data sequencing rules; and much, much more. Breakout sessions for industry, jurisdictional, and technical participants will be held to offer information specific to each group.</p> 
        <p><strong><a href="https://www.iaiabc.org/assnfe/ev.asp?ID=533&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;utm_campaign=EDI%20Claims%2031%20Training%20Tennessee%20Reminder&amp;utm_content=EDI%20Claims%2031%20Training%20Tennessee%20Reminder+Preview+CID_6193b64fc752bb64084392770cd0aac2&amp;utm_source=Email%20marketing%20software&amp;utm_term=EDI%20Claims%20Release%2031%20face-to-face%20training" target="_blank">Registration</a></strong> is $325 for IAIABC members and $475 for non-members.</p> 
        <p><em>Please note: EDI or Jurisdictional&nbsp;members with an active 2019 training subscription will receive a coupon for $50 off each registration. Please do not register without code as price adjustments will not be made post-registration.</em></p> 
       </div>
