           <h1>Contact the Office of the Federal Register</h1> 
           <p><img align="right" alt="Office of the Federal Register" class="floatRight noBorder" height="191" src="/files/federal-register/images/fedreg-door.jpg" width="250"> <a name="hours"></a> Our office is open:</p> 
           <ul> 
            <li>8:45 a.m. to 5:15 p.m. Eastern Time</li> 
            <li>Monday through Friday</li> 
            <li>We are closed on Federal Holidays.<br> &nbsp;</li> 
           </ul>

           <div class="floatRight width45 spaceAbove spaceLeft smaller"> 
            <div class="nwidget layout block title-box clearfix " data-additional-classes="" data-title="Questions about Rules &amp; Regulations"> 
             <h2>Questions about Rules &amp; Regulations</h2> 
             <p>Please contact the agency that issued the document if you have specific questions about any document that appears in the:</p> 
             <ul> 
              <li><a href="/federal-register/the-federal-register/"><em>Federal Register</em></a></li> 
              <li><a href="/federal-register/cfr/"><em>Code of Federal Regulations</em></a></li> 
             </ul> 
             <hr> 
             <ul class="lines"> 
              <li><a class="" href="/federal-register/the-federal-register/about.html#questions"> <i class="fa fa-envelope">&nbsp;</i>How to Contact the Agency </a></li> 
             </ul> 
            </div> 
           </div> 
           <p><a name="location"></a> <strong>Location </strong> (<em>See our <a href="/federal-register/contact.html#coop">COOP Location</a></em>)<br> &nbsp;</p> 
           <p><strong><em>We moved on May 15, 2017. &nbsp;Take note of the new address.</em></strong></p> 
           <p>Our office is located two blocks north of the Union Station Metro, at:</p> 
           <p>7 G Street, NW<br> Suite A-734<br> Washington DC 20401</p> 
           <p><strong>Use our G Street address for:</strong></p> 
           <ul> 
            <li>Pick-up and delivery by messenger or overnight carrier</li> 
            <li>Viewing documents on <a href="http://www.federalregister.gov/public-inspection/">Public Inspection</a></li> 
            <li>Appointments with our staff</li> 
           </ul> 
           <p>Do not address <a href="#usmail">U.S. Mail</a> to 7 G Street</p>
