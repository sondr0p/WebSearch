      <hr class="blue">

         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> Agency mailing and physical addresses, and phone numbers can be found on their web sites.         </div> 
        <div id="collapseTwo" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> You can find telephone numbers for some State employees, agencies and type of service provided by State agencies in the <a href="/phonedirectory/" title="State Phone Directory">State Phone Directory</a>. </p>

        <div class="panel-heading"> 
         <p class="panel-title"> <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion2" href="#collapseThree"><strong><i class="fa fa-arrow-right fa-lg"></i> &nbsp; What do I need to bring when applying for or renewing my driver's license or ID card?</strong></a> </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="collapseThree" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> On July 1, 2010, the Delaware Division of Motor Vehicles (DMV) began offering secure driver licenses and identification cards. A list of documents you will need to COLLECT and BRING with you so you can SECURE your new driver license or identification card can be found at <a href="http://www.dmv.de.gov/services/driver_services/drivers_license/dr_lic_secure_dl.shtml" title="Drivers License/ID Card" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.dmv.de.gov/services/driver_services/drivers_license/dr_lic_secure_dl.shtml'); return false;">dmv.de.gov</a> </p> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div>

      <div class="panel-group" id="accordion3"> 
       <div class="panel programs_panel_blue"> 
        <div class="panel-heading"> 
         <p class="panel-title"> <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion3" href="#collapseFour"><strong><i class="fa fa-arrow-right fa-lg"></i> &nbsp; How do I form a business entity in Delaware?</strong></a> </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="collapseFour" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> The Division of Corporations' web site at <a href="http://corp.delaware.gov/howtoform.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://corp.delaware.gov/howtoform.shtml'); return false;">corp.delaware.gov</a> gives a step by step procedure on forming a business entity in Delaware. These entities, whether located in Delaware or outside the State, include Corporations, Public Benefit Corporations (effective August 1, 2013), Limited Liability Companies (LLC), Limited Partnerships (LP), Statutory Trusts and many General Partnerships (GP). </p> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div> 
      <br> 
      <div class="panel-group" id="accordion4"> 
       <div class="panel programs_panel"> 
        <div class="panel-heading"> 
         <p class="panel-title"> <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion4" href="#collapseFive"><strong><i class="fa fa-arrow-right fa-lg"></i> &nbsp; How do I register my business in Delaware?</strong></a> </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="collapseFive" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> You can register your business with the Division of Revenue, the Division of Unemployment Insurance and the Office of Workers Compensation at the State of Delaware's <a href="https://onestop.delaware.gov/osbrlpublic/Home.jsp" title="One Stop Registration" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://onestop.delaware.gov/osbrlpublic/Home.jsp'); return false;">One Stop Business Registration and Licensing System</a>. Professional firms must, in addition to obtaining a business license, apply with the <a href="http://www.dpr.delaware.gov/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.dpr.delaware.gov/'); return false;">Division of Professional Regulations</a> for a license to practice. </p> 
         </div> 
        </div> 
       </div> 
      </div>

        <div class="panel-heading"> 
         <p class="panel-title"> <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion5" href="#collapseSix"><strong><i class="fa fa-arrow-right fa-lg"></i> &nbsp; Can I file my taxes online?</strong></a> </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="collapseSix" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> <a href="http://revenue.delaware.gov/pit_onlinefiling.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://revenue.delaware.gov/pit_onlinefiling.shtml'); return false;">Personal Income Taxes</a>, for both current and 3 prior years, can be filed online. <a href="https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/indpay/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/indpay/'); return false;">Individual Tax Payments</a>, <a href="https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/pitest/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/pitest/'); return false;">Estimated Tax Payments</a> and <a href="https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/scripts/olp/olp.dll/pitext" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/scripts/olp/olp.dll/pitext'); return false;">Requests for an Extension</a> can also be made online. </p> 
          <p> Delaware currently does not accept the filing of corporate income tax return online. However, <a href="http://www.grossreceiptstax.delaware.gov/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.grossreceiptstax.delaware.gov/'); return false;">Gross Receipts Tax</a> can be filed and paid online and a <a href="https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/scripts/olp/olp.dll/buspay" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://dorweb.revenue.delaware.gov/scripts/olp/olp.dll/buspay'); return false;">balance due</a> on withholding, gross receipts, license, or corporate income tax can be paid online. Business Tax Forms can be found on the Division of Revenue's site at <a href="http://revenue.delaware.gov/services/Business_Tax/Forms_New.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://revenue.delaware.gov/services/Business_Tax/Forms_New.shtml'); return false;">revenue.delaware.gov</a>. </p> 
          <p> Corporate Franchise Tax and Annual Reports can be filed/paid online at <a href="http://corp.delaware.gov/paytaxes.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://corp.delaware.gov/paytaxes.shtml'); return false;">corp.delaware.gov</a>. Also at this link, Limited Partnerships, Limited Liability Companies and General Partnerships formed or registered in Delaware can pay their annual tax of $250.00. </p> 
          <p> Bank Franchise Tax cannot be filed/paid online. The State Bank Commissioner's site has instructions and forms on their site at <a href="http://banking.delaware.gov/bnkft/ftintro.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://banking.delaware.gov/bnkft/ftintro.shtml'); return false;">banking.delaware.gov</a>. </p> 
         </div>

         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> Marriage licenses can be obtained in the Clerk of the Peace's Office in each county. You should apply for the license in the county where you will be married. For contact information, links to their websites can be found on our <a href="/topics/marriage/">Delaware Marriage</a> topic page. </p>

         <p class="panel-title"> <a data-toggle="collapse" data-parent="#accordion8" href="#collapseNine"><strong><i class="fa fa-arrow-right fa-lg"></i> &nbsp; When is a homeowner required to obtain a building permit?</strong></a> </p> 
        </div> 
        <div id="collapseNine" class="panel-collapse collapse"> 
         <div class="panel-body"> 
          <p> Homeowners should apply for a permit only if they are doing the home improvement project themselves. If a contractor is doing the work, it is their responsibility to obtain the permit.
