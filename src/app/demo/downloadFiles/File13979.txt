       <p>All forms are <strong>FREE</strong>. Not all forms are listed. If you can't find the form you need, or you need help completing a form, please call us at <strong>1-800-772-1213 (TTY 1-800-325-0778)</strong> or contact your <a href="/locator/">local Social Security office</a> and we will help you. If you download, print and complete a paper form, please mail or take it to your <a href="/locator/">local Social Security office</a> or the office that requested it from you.</p> 
       <p>Note: If using a screen reader, we suggest opening the form in Adobe Reader. If you do not have Adobe Reader, please go to the following <a href="https://get.adobe.com/reader/" title="Adobe Reader Link" target="new">link</a>. Thank you!</p>
