      <h2><a href="https://denotificationservices.bbcportal.com" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://denotificationservices.bbcportal.com'); return false;">Delaware Notification Service (DNS)</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>After you sign up, the Delaware Notification Service (DNS) lets you select which notifications, news, and information you'd like to receive by E-mail. For select services, you can also receive notifications by a phone call, text message, and/or fax. DNS notifications include:</p> 
      <ul> 
       <li>School Closings and Delays (including early dismissals)</li> 
       <li>Statewide School-Related Announcements</li> 
       <li>Information from the Public Archives, including "This Day in Delaware" Daily E-mails</li>
       <!-- <li>Press releases from the Governor's Office</li> --> 
       <li>Information on the FirstMap online GIS system</li> 
       <li>Sex Offender Status Changes</li> 
       <li>Cyber Security Updates from the Department of Technology and Information</li> 
       <li>Regulatory Actions</li> 
       <li>Legislative Actions</li> 
       <li>Vendor Notifications</li> 
       <li>Delaware Code Notifications</li> 
       <li>Auditor of Accounts News Releases</li> 
       <li> Auditor of Accounts Reports</li> 
      </ul>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="https://www.deldot.gov/mydeldot.ejs?command=MyDelDOTNew" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://www.deldot.gov/mydeldot.ejs?command=MyDelDOTNew'); return false;">MyDelDOT</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>Sign up to receive information directly from the Delaware Department of Transportation regarding traffic conditions and press releases.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="http://apps.dnrec.state.de.us/derns/pub/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://apps.dnrec.state.de.us/derns/pub/'); return false;">Delaware Environmental Release Notification System</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>In response to Senate Bill 33, which became law in July 2001, the Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Control (DNREC) developed this system to help Delawareans learn promptly of releases or discharges of contaminants or pollutants that meet or exceed certain thresholds in their neighborhoods or throughout the state.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="https://lists.state.de.us/read/all_forums/subscribe?name=dgdc-l" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://lists.state.de.us/read/all_forums/subscribe?name=dgdc-l'); return false;">Delaware Geographic Data Committee (DGDC) List</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>The DGDC List is designed to allow the Delaware GIS Community to share information related to spatial data and the Geographic Information System (GIS) software tools that use that data. It is an open list. Anyone may join this list by using the on-line form located on the DGDC List web page.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="http://www.delottery.com/playersclub.asp" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://www.delottery.com/playersclub.asp'); return false;">Delaware Lottery Players Club</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>Sign up now to receive Latest News and Winning Numbers sent directly to you from the Delaware Lottery.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="http://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/state.shtml#listserve" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/state.shtml#listserve'); return false;">Delaware Surplus Services</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>The Office of Management and Budget, Government Support Services, provides an email list to inform subscribers of available excess State property, special items for purchase via bid, notification of their Vehicle and Heavy Equipment Auctions, and notification of online auctions.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="http://dnrec.alpha.delaware.gov/dnrec-e-mail-lists/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://dnrec.alpha.delaware.gov/dnrec-e-mail-lists/'); return false;">DNREC Direct</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>The Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Control provides email lists to enable interested citizens to receive information from the department as quickly as possible regarding the following topics: Beach Monitoring, Biodiversity, Contracts, Enforcement Actions, Fisheries, Metachem, Mosquito Control Spraying, Observer, Ozone Alerts, Press Releases, Public Notices, and Wildlife.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="https://lists.state.de.us/read/all_forums/subscribe?name=planning" onclick="trackOutboundLink('https://lists.state.de.us/read/all_forums/subscribe?name=planning'); return false;">Planning News List</a></h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p>The Planning News List is designed to share information about planning-related issues, meetings and news in Delaware. It is an open list. Anyone may join this list by using the on-line form located on the web page of The Planning News List.</p>
      <br> 
      <h2><a href="http://hrm.omb.delaware.gov/closings/emailnotification.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink(http://hrm.omb.delaware.gov/closings/emailnotification.shtml'); return false;">State of Delaware Closing/Delay Email Notification</a></h2>
