          <section id="block-system-main" class="block block-system clearfix"> 
           <p><strong><a href="/presidential-libraries/about">Learn about Presidential Libraries and Museums</a></strong> <span class="floatLeft"> <img alt="Presidential Libraries" border="0" height="79" src="/files/presidential-libraries/images/logo/nl-logo.gif" vspace="7" width="89"></span> <span class="smaller"><br> Presidential Libraries and Museums promote understanding of the presidency and the American experience. We preserve and provide access to historical materials, support research, and create interactive programs and exhibits that educate and inspire.</span></p> 
           <p><strong><a href="/presidential-libraries/visit">Visit the Presidential Libraries and Museums</a></strong><span class="smaller"><br> Presidential Libraries and Museums present vast archives of documents, museums full of important Presidential artifacts, interesting educational and public programs, and informative web sites.</span></p> 
           <p><strong><a href="/presidential-libraries/research">Research Presidential Documents</a></strong> <span class="smaller"><br> Presidential Libraries and Museums are repositories for the papers, records and historical materials of the Presidents. We work to ensure that these irreplaceable items are preserved and made available for the widest possible use by researchers.</span></p> 
           <p><a href="/presidential-libraries/events"><strong>Explore Public and Education Programs</strong></a> <span class="smaller"><br> Presidential Libraries and Museums give you the chance to see, hear, and participate in the events that changed our lives and made us who we are as a nation.</span></p>
