        <p>Use our APIs (application programming interfaces) to seamlessly add healthfinder content (Health Topics A to Z or personalized recommendations on clinical preventive services) to your website or blog, or to create a unique tool supported by our trusted health content.</p> 
        <p>All of the original disease prevention content of the <a href="/HealthTopics/">Health Topics A to Z</a> section and the evidence-based <a href="/myhealthfinder/">Clinical Preventive Services Recommendations</a> are available in both English and Spanish.</p> 
        <p>To access the APIs, register using the form to the right. A confirmation that includes your unique API key will be sent to your email address. Please save the email for future reference.</p> 
        <p>To preview and explore the API, check out the <a href="/Developer/Documentation/">interactive API documentation</a>.</p>
