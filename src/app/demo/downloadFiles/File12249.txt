    <h1 class="visible-xs"> Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) </h1> 
    <br> 
    <div class="row"> 
     <div class="col-sm-8"> 
      <h2> About the Act </h2> 
      <hr class="blue"> 
      <p> <strong>Delaware Freedom of Information Act</strong><br> The Act ( <a href="http://delcode.delaware.gov/title29/c100/index.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://delcode.delaware.gov/title29/c100/index.shtml'); return false;">29 Del. Code, Chapter 100)</a> establishes that "it is vital in a democratic society that public business be performed in an open and public manner" and that "it is vital that citizens have easy access to public records in order that the society remain free and democratic." </p> 
      <p> <strong>Senate Bill 87</strong><br> Passed in the 146th General Assembly and signed by Governor Markell on October 20, 2011, <a href="http://delcode.delaware.gov/sessionlaws/ga146/chp202.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://delcode.delaware.gov/sessionlaws/ga146/chp202.shtml'); return false;">requires that a standard form for requests</a> under the Act be made available to the public. </p> 
      <p> <strong>Executive Order No. 31</strong><br> <a href="http://governor.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/24/2016/12/EO031.pdf" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://governor.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/24/2016/12/EO031.pdf'); return false;">Approved by the Governor</a> on October 20, 2011, requires executive branch agencies to adopt a standardized policy for requests. </p> 
      <p> <strong>Frequently Asked Questions </strong><br> The Delaware Department of Justice provides <a href="http://attorneygeneral.delaware.gov/executive/open-government/" onclick="trackOutboundLink('http://attorneygeneral.delaware.gov/executive/open-government/'); return false;">answers to common questions about the Freedom of Information Act </a> on its website. </p>
      <p> <strong>State Agency FOIA Coordinators</strong><br> <a href="/help/AgencyListFOIACoordinators-table.shtml" onclick="trackOutboundLink('/help/AgencyListFOIACoordinators-table.shtml'); return false;">List of Coordinators</a> </p>

      <p> <strong>NOTICE</strong> :Under Delaware's Freedom of Information Act, 29 Del. C. §§ 10001-10006 ("FOIA"), a FOIA request or petition, along with any information contained therein or any documents attached thereto, submitted to any "public body" subject to FOIA, including, without limitation, any board, bureau, commission, department, agency or committee of the State, may itself be deemed a "public record" subject to disclosure under FOIA. </p>
